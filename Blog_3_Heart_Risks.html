Heart Health Risks

High blood pressure, high cholesterol, diabetes, and smoking are among the most common risks for heart disease.

Unhealthy lifestyles, including lack of exercise, poor diet, and excessive alcohol consumption, can amplify these risks.

Early detection and proactive management can mitigate risks significantly. Regular check-ups and healthier choices make a difference.
