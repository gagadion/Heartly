Our Story

Heartly is a personal initiative by Gargi from NPS Rajajinagar. Growing up, I witnessed the pain of losing close ones to heart attacks.

Many people lack the knowledge to act fast during emergencies—administering CPR or using an AED could have saved lives.

Through Heartly, I aim to spread awareness, educate people, and ensure that critical heart health resources are accessible to everyone.
