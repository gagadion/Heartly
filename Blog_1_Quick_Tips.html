5 Quick Tips for a Healthy Heart

5 Quick Tips

1. Stay Active: Engage in at least 30 minutes of physical activity daily to maintain heart health.

2. Eat Right: Include vegetables, fruits, whole grains, and lean proteins in your diet.

3. Manage Stress: Practice mindfulness and relaxation techniques to reduce stress.

4. Avoid Smoking: Protect your heart and lungs by quitting smoking.

5. Get Regular Checkups: Consult your doctor for routine heart health monitoring.
