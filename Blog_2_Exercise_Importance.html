The Importance of Regular Exercise

Regular Exercise and Your Heart

Physical activity is one of the best ways to protect your heart. Regular exercise strengthens the heart muscle, improves blood circulation, and lowers blood pressure.

Simple activities like walking, jogging, swimming, or cycling for 30 minutes a day can significantly improve your cardiovascular health.

Consistency is key. Make exercise a daily habit to reap lifelong heart health benefits.
